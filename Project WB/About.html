Biography

King Nassar (Nassar Sungkar) is one of Indonesia’s most popular dangdut singers and entertainers. Born in Bandung on January 5, 1988, he gained national recognition after participating in Kontes Dangdut Indonesia (KDI). With his powerful voice and charming personality, Nassar quickly became one of the most beloved figures in the Indonesian music industry.

Known for his signature phrase “Alhamdulillah ya, kan!” and captivating stage performances, King Nassar continues to inspire fans through his music, humor, and authenticity. Beyond his singing career, he is also active as a television host and reality show judge, bringing joy and entertainment to audiences across the country.
